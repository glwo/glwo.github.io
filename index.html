About Me

Resume

Hello, I'm Glen! I'm a software engineer with a strong passion for problem-solving and app development. I developed my skillset through App Academy's immersive boot camp while honing my skills through multiple full-stack projects and rigorous coding challenges.

Prior to this, I studied integrative physiology at CU in hopes of becoming a physical therapist for an NHL team. My passion transitioned from exercise science to coding during the pandemic when I took a statistics course that involved sorting health data using code written in R-studio. I found the workflow of coding much more self-expressive and rewarding compared to my previous health courses. As a lifelong learner, I always strive to keep up with the latest technologies and best practices within the industry.

When I'm not coding, I enjoy staying active through various outdoor activities and weightlifting.
